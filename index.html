About Ahoada East LGA

Welcome to Our Local Government Area

Ahoada East Local Government Area, located in Rivers State, Nigeria, is a vibrant community known for its rich cultural heritage and natural resources. Established in 1991, our LGA has been at the forefront of grassroots development and community engagement.

Key facts about Ahoada East LGA:

  • Population: Approximately 200,000 (as of 2021 estimate)
  • Land Area: 341 kmÂ²
  • Headquarters: Ahoada
  • Major Towns: Ahoada, Edeoha, Ula-Ehuda, Ogbele

Our LGA is committed to sustainable development, quality education, and improved healthcare for all residents. We strive to create an enabling environment for businesses to thrive and for our youth to reach their full potential.
